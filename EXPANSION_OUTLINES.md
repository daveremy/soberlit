- **Week 3-4 breakthroughs:**

  - Social comfort improving (scripts feel natural now)
- **Specific struggles:**

- **What I'm learning:** The "dopamine reset" is realâ€”things that felt "meh" at day 10 feel genuinely fun by day 25

**Phase 2: Days 31-60 (Build Capacity)** (1.5 pages)
- **What I'm doing:** Movement progressing, nutrition dialed in, relationships deepening
- **Month 2 shifts:**
  - No longer counting days obsessively
  - "Unfuckwithable" feeling emerging: alcohol in fridge doesn't trigger me
  - Deeper conversations with partner (presence is different)
- **Challenges:**
  - Travel/vacation test (cruise): brought NA options, used scripts, didn't feel deprived
  - Boredom still surfaces but solving it is now automatic (walk, call friend, hobby)
- **For AI:** Goal refinement, celebrate milestones, adjust strategies

**Phase 3: Days 61-90 (Deepen & Integrate)** (1.5 pages)
- **What I'm doing:** New hobby integrated, relationships feel richer, planning post-90 maintenance
- **Month 3 insights:**
  - This is just "how I live now"â€”not a project with an end date

- **Looking forward:** What does year 1 look like? How do I maintain without rigidity?
- **For AI:** Long-term vision, ongoing check-ins, adaptive planning

**Closing (0.5 page):**
- "This was my path. Yours will have different milestones, different struggles, different wins."
- "The frameworks (HALT, trigger swaps, social scripts) are tools you can adapt. The evidence (Part II) is your 'why.' Your daily choices are the 'how.'"

**1. The 6 PM Spritz (Replaces Wine)**

- 2 oz tonic

- Ice, wine glass

**2. The Ginger Zinger (Replaces Cocktail)**
- 6 oz ginger beer
- 1 oz fresh lime juice
- Fresh mint, muddled
- Ice, rocks glass
- **When:** Social events, "I want something interesting"
- **Why:** Complex flavor, looks like a drink, conversation starter

**3. Tart Cherry Sleep Aid (Replaces Nightcap)**
- 4 oz tart cherry juice
- 4 oz warm water
- Optional: chamomile tea bag steeped
- **When:** Pre-bed ritual
- **Why:** Natural melatonin, warm liquid = sleep cue

**4. The Energizer (Replaces "Pick Me Up" Drink)**
- 6 oz sparkling water
- 1 oz kombucha (ginger or citrus)
- Squeeze of lemon
- Ice
- **When:** Afternoon slump, when you'd reach for wine to "perk up"
- **Why:** Probiotics, effervescence, ritual without sedation

**5. The Fancy Guest (Social Armor)**
- 3 oz Pellegrino
- 3 oz grapefruit juice
- Rosemary sprig
- Ice, tall glass with garnish
- **When:** Hosting or being hosted
- **Why:** Looks sophisticated, starts conversations, you're not explaining

**For AI:** Recipe suggestions based on time of day, mood, social context

**Section 3: Rituals That Honor the Moment** (1.5 pages)

**The 6 PM Ritual (Replaces Wine O'Clock):**
- Set the table, even if it's just you
- Use a nice glass (the wine glass is fine!)
- Make the drink with intention (measure, muddle, garnish)
- Sit down, don't stand at the counter
- Sip slowly, savor it
- **Why:** You're honoring the ritual of "marking the end of the day" without alcohol

**The Social Pour (Hosting/Being Hosted):**
- Bring your own NA options (don't rely on host)
- Offer to "make drinks" for othersâ€”normalize the ritual
- Have your script ready ("I'm on an alcohol-free streak")
- **Why:** Control + contribution = confidence

**The Stress Interrupt (When Urge Hits Hard):**
- Pause: physical movement (walk outside, 10 squats, cold water on face)
- HALT check: What's the real need?
- Make a drink (one of the 5 above) OR
- Eat something (protein + fat combo)
- **Why:** Breaks the autopilot; addresses root need
