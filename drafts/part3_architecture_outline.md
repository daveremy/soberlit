## Chapter 1: The Mortality Paradox — The Clock is the Ultimate Reality

**Key Points:**
- **The Math for Men:** Male life expectancy vs. healthspan. The window of high-performance years is finite.
- **Stoicism for the Modern Man:**
  - *Memento Mori* isn't poetry; it's operational reality.

- **The "Ageless" Mindset:** Not trying to look 30, but maintaining the *capacity* to act with agency until the very end.

## Chapter 3: We Are All Athletes — Preserving the Machine

**Core Concept:** Your body is the only vehicle you get. For men, physical capacity is directly tied to independence and dignity.

**Key Points:**
- **Testosterone & Physiology:**
  - Alcohol tanks testosterone (aromatase activity).
  - Strength training boosts it.
  - The choice is binary: accelerate the "grumpy old man" decline (low T, high cortisol) or fight for vitality.
- **"Motion is Medicine":**
  - Sarcopenia (muscle loss) is the enemy of the older man.
  - Strength is independence. (Can you lift your own luggage? Can you pick up your grandchild?)
- **The Integrated Athlete:**
  - Not chasing PRs or ego.
  - Training for *utility* and *longevity*.
  - Movement is the primary tool for regulating male mood.
