# Spirituality & Mindfulness (Choose What Fits You)

> Offer options; no prescription. Aim: calm, meaning, connection.

## Options
- Mindfulness/meditation: 5–10 minutes daily; breath focus or body scan.
- Prayer/reflection: set times (morning/evening); gratitude list.
- Nature: walks outdoors; mindful attention to surroundings.
- Community: faith groups, meditation groups, or values-based gatherings.

## How to use
- Pair brief practice with morning or evening anchor.
- Use as a stress/urge interrupt: 3–5 minutes of breath or gratitude when triggered.
- Keep it personal—choose practices that feel authentic.
