# Audit 9: The Filter (Liver & Gut)

> **The Mechanism:** Hepatic Stress
> **The Cost:** Your Metabolism

Your liver is the oil filter of the human machine.
It performs 500+ functions. It regulates energy, hormones, clotting, and toxins.

When you drink, you force this critical organ to drop everything else and focus entirely on damage control.

## The Priority Problem

Alcohol is a poison. Your body knows this.
When alcohol enters the system, your liver stops burning fat. It stops processing other nutrients. It goes into "All Hands on Deck" mode to metabolize the acetaldehyde before it kills cells.

**Result:** Everything else gets backed up. Fat accumulates.

## Fatty Liver: The Silent Epidemic

You don't need to be a "drunk" to have fatty liver.
**Alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease** is common in "moderate" drinkers.
It means your liver cells are being replaced by fat.

A fatty liver is a sluggish liver. It leads to:
*   Fatigue.
*   Brain fog.
*   Poor digestion.
*   Hormonal imbalances (high estrogen).

## The Integrated Man Angle: Maintenance

You change the oil in your car. You clean the filters in your HVAC.
Why are you clogging the most important filter you own?

The liver is incredibly resilient. It is the only organ that can fully regenerate.
**If you stop assaulting it.**

Give it a break. Let it clear the fat. Let it get back to its real job: powering your life.
